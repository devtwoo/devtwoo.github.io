About Us

Kuwait Humanitarian Society charity was established in Kuwait. Declared under Ministerial Decree No. (72 /A) 2016 and in accordance with the provisions of Law No. 24 Year 1962 of the Ministry of Social Affairs and Labor

The Mission

Our mission are to help people in need and generate resources through fundraising and use those funds to make an impact in the lives of people in need.

  • Strengthen Kuwait's role as a global center for humanity worldwide
    1
  • Highlight the positive image of Kuwaiti humanitarian work worldwide.
    2
  • Highlight the tolerance of Islam and the bright image of Islam's authentic human values.
    3
  • Disseminating the intermediate curriculum that respects human dignity sensually and morally.
    4
  • Highlighting the images of Islam's care for humanity in various fields of caring for needy families and orphans.

    Conducting studies, researches, conferences and specialized courses in the development of humanitarian work and publications and media and cultural exhibitions related to the objectives of the Association.
